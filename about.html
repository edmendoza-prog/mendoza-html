Welcome to About Us!

At Gunsmiths Guide, we’re passionate gamers dedicated to helping the Call of Duty: Mobile community get the most out of their weapons. Our mission is simple: provide clear, detailed, and easy-to-understand information about every Gunsmith attachment so players can build the perfect loadouts for their playstyle.


Whether you’re a beginner learning the ropes or a seasoned veteran chasing the ultimate setup, our goal is to make your weapon customization process faster, smarter, and more effective. We analyze attachments, test combinations, and share strategies so you can focus on what matters—dominating the battlefield.


We believe that every attachment counts, and every player deserves the knowledge to play at their best. Join us, level up your game, and make every match count.
